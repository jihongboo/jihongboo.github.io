Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

FluxTV does not collect any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Channel Sources - M3U/M3U8 playlist URLs you add to the app
  • App Preferences - Display settings such as appearance mode and auto-play preference
  • Recently Watched - Channel history for the Top Shelf feature (tvOS only)

Data Not Collected

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data transmitted to third-party services

Third-Party Services

FluxTV connects to streaming URLs that you provide. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party streaming services or content providers.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally using Apple secure frameworks (SwiftData and UserDefaults). Data is protected by your device security features including encryption.

Children Privacy

FluxTV does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
