FluxTV Support

IPTV streaming app for Apple devices

Getting Started

FluxTV allows you to watch IPTV streams by adding M3U/M3U8 playlist URLs. To get started:

  • Open FluxTV and go to Sources
  • Tap Add Source and enter your M3U playlist URL
  • Your channels will be automatically imported and organized by category

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I get M3U playlists?

M3U playlists are typically provided by your IPTV service provider. FluxTV is a player only and does not provide any streaming content.

A channel is not playing. What should I do?

This usually means the stream URL is offline or invalid. Try refreshing your source, or contact your IPTV provider to verify the stream is active.

Which platforms are supported?

FluxTV supports iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.

Is my data synced across devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on each device. iCloud sync may be added in a future update.
